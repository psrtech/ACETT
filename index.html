Welcome

To ACE Table Tennis Academy

ACE Table Tennis Academy, established in January 2025 on Water Canal Road, Kolathur, is an extension of the Villivakkam branch, which has been nurturing talent since 2010. We offer top-tier coaching for all skill levels, focusing on technique, agility, and competitive strategy.

  • 100+ students trained, fostering talent across all levels.
  • Produces 2-3 national players every year through expert coaching.
  • Provides dedicated fitness training for strength, agility, and endurance.
  • Equipped with advanced facilities, including multi-ball training and video analysis.

Join us to train with expert coaches, refine your skills, and compete at the highest level!

ACE Table Tennis Academy (ACE TT) is a premier branch of the Singaram Pillai Table Tennis Club, located in Villivakkam, Chennai. Known for its professional coaching and personalized training programs, ACE TT continues the legacy of the Singaram Pillai Club, which has been fostering table tennis talent since its establishment in 2010. Catering to players of all levels, the academy provides a structured curriculum to help both beginners and advanced athletes excel in the sport. Falcon Sports and Event Managers, the organization overseeing both ACE TT and Singaram Pillai Club, plays a pivotal role in maintaining the quality and reputation of these institutions. With the launch of the new ACE TT branch in Surapet in 2024, Falcon continues its mission to promote table tennis excellence and expand the sport's reach within the community.
